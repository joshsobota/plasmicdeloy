Who is Fathym?

We created Fathym to help address the imbalance in software development. Over the years, there has been a steady progression of removing the complex tasks that only a handful of senior engineers can tackle. Fathym plays a crucial role in this progression. We help diverse groups of people rapidly build and deploy cloud-native applications saving you time and money.
Our Technology
LowCodeUnit
LowCodeUnit operates as a virtual development team that automates best practices for application delivery. To help you focus on building, we built LowCodeUnit to augment your team’s skills and do the heavy lifting.
IoT Ensemble
With IoT Ensemble, you can enroll devices and get IoT data flowing to downstream alerts, visualizations, and machine learning applications in minutes.
pROADject
Drive safely knowing road conditions and weather along your route.Use Proadject to help you determine which routes to take on trips. Proadject uses the Habistack Forecast APIs to retrieve the ground level, route-based weather forecast.
Habistack
Built with LowCodeUnit, Habistack provides ground level, route-based weather forecast APIs for anywhere in the United States. Outside of the continental United States we provide lower resolution forecasts.
